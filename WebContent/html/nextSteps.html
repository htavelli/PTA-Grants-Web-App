Timeline and Next Steps
  • After the grant cycle closes, the grant committee will meet and decide on grants to approve as quickly as possible.
  • You will receive a reply to the email address provided during the application process to let you know whether your grant was approved.
  • If you are awarded a grant, you may either purchase items yourself and submit receipts to the PTA for reimbursement, or request a check from the PTA. Please allow one to two weeks for either process.
  • As a not-for-profit, the PTA is unable to pay sales tax. Obtain a tax-exempt form from the office before making your purchases as PTA will be unable to reimburse you for it.
  • All grant funds must be used by the last school day of the year or the money is forfeited.
General PTA Information

The Parent Teacher Association at is a not-for-profit organization. The funds provided for teacher grants come from the total budget of the PTA. Those funds are a result of the fundraising efforts of our families along with membership dues. The PTA is committed to its alliance with the school staff to make the best environment for our children to learn, grow, and excel as they learn essential skills for the future.
